Dice Wars

Roll the dice and score points!

About Game & Rules

In this game, two players take turns to roll a dice. The player who rolls the highest number wins the round and scores points. The first player to reach 50 points wins the game!
Rules:
1. Roll the dice by clicking the "ROLL DICE" button.
2. Hold your score by clicking the "PASS" button.
3. Restart the game anytime by clicking the "RESTART" button.
Good luck and have fun!
